Professional Summary

Despite having grown up in the Province of Tucumán, from 2007 to the beginning of 2019 I lived and developed my academic and professional life in the Province of Córdoba. My first jobs were at the beginning of my academic life, as a scholarship holder at the Process Simulation Laboratory, programming industrial process simulation models in Visual Logic. This was my first approach into the world of programming. Some time later I entered the fascinating world of the Automotive Industry, where I developed myself as an Engineer for 6 years. First at Magneti Marelli SPA (FCA group company), and then for Renault-Nissan. These two multinational companies were an exceptional business and project management school for me and were a breaking point in my way of seeing the world and business. The necesity of performing complex analysis using tools like Ms Excel led me to venture into Visual Basic in a self-taught way, a language that is very similar to Visual Logic.
In 2015 I started myself in the entrepreneurial world, but with the clear objective of founding small businesses, developing their internal structures and selling them short to obtain a profit. So it was that in three years I co-founded three startups (all three are still standing) and it was in the development of their internal structures that I discovered my passion for software. In all three cases I developed their management and POS systems in VisualBasic.
In 2019 I decided to change course, return for a time to my province and dedicate myself to deeply develope my knowledge and skills as a software programmer. So, last year I took a Introductive Python course (thanks to my Python skills I am currently serving as a freelance engineer to the Public Ministry of my province), and during the course of this year I took the Fullstack Developer course. This is a beginning, and I am very happy to finally found my passion.
